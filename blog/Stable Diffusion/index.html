使用Stable Diffusion1.0模型,本地生成图片

发表于2024-02-05|更新于2024-02-21|Share
|字数总计:1.6k|阅读时长:8分钟|阅读量:

简介

SDXL1.0模型是Stability AI 推出的一款免费图像生成模型，输出的图像分辨率提高到了1024×1024，简化了步骤,让文生图变得更为便捷高效,几句话就可以输出美观的图像。
项目地址：https://github.com/Stability-AI/generative-models
模型下载：
base model ：https://huggingface.co/stabilityai/stable-diffusion-xl-base-1.0
refiner model：https://huggingface.co/stabilityai/stable-diffusion-xl-refiner-1.0
ComfyUI：https://github.com/comfyanonymous/ComfyUI
Windows Version：https://github.com/comfyanonymous/ComfyUI/releases/download/latest/ComfyUI_windows_portable_nvidia_cu121_or_cpu.7z
在线体验：https://stability.ai/
模型大小：6~7GB
推荐配置：6GB或以上显存的NVIDIA显卡,1GB/s或以上的硬盘读写速度。

用法

首先下载ConfyUI(windows用户直接下载整合包)和模型,解压后把模型放入主目录中的ComfyUI➡models➡checkpoints 文件夹中,例如：G:\Project\Stable Diffusion1.0\ComfyUI_windows_portable\ComfyUI\models\checkpoints
英伟达显卡运行run_nvidia_gpu.bat,没有可以运行run_cpu.bat(显存不足自动开启低显存模式),稍等片刻自动打开网页程序


可以看到图形界面有正面提示词和负面提示词,可以运行默认的提示词先看看效果,点击Queue Prompt生成,第一次出图一般都是下面这张：

注意第一次使用要先加载模型,加载速度和硬盘读写速度以及设备性能有关。如果配置较低请耐心等待，并没有卡住,只是比较慢罢了。
在Empty Latent Image 下可以修改生成的图像大小,最大像素为1024×1024，默认512×512
在Ksample下可以修改步数和CFG值(classifier-free guidance scale),并不是越高越好,可自行摸索。
图像生成后自动保存到ComfyUI下的output文件夹中,也可以右键图像,自定义存储位置。
想要生成复杂的图形一定要多加一些提示词,可以自行谷歌相关模板。

视频教程


视频的后半段为stable-diffusion-webui的安装教程,配置要求更高,显存≥12GB推荐试一试,可以安装额外的插件,更为方便。

提示词

能否生成好的图像很大程度上取决于提示词的用法,下面是一些样本可供参考
来源：SDXL 1.0 多种风格高质量prompt

Generic Starter Prompt通用提示词
Prompt ：breathtaking {prompt} . award-winning, professional, highly detailed
Negative ：anime, cartoon, graphic, text, painting, crayon, graphite, abstract glitch, blurry
示例:
Prompt ：breathtaking selfie photograph of astronaut floating in space, earth in the background. award-winning, professional, highly detailed
Negative ：anime, cartoon, graphic, text, painting, crayon, graphite, abstract glitch, blurry

Anime动画风格:
Prompt ：anime artwork {prompt} . anime style, key visual, vibrant, studio anime, highly detailed
Negative :photo, deformed, black and white, realism, disfigured, low contrast
示例：
Prompt ：anime artwork an empty classroom. anime style, key visual, vibrant, studio anime, highly detailed
Negative ：photo, deformed, black and white, realism, disfigured, low contrast

Digital Art / Concept Art 数字艺术/概念艺术:
Prompt ：concept art {prompt}. digital artwork, illustrative, painterly, matte painting, highly detailed
Negative :photo, photorealistic, realism, ugly
示例：
Prompt ：concept art of a warrior with a sword, clouds. digital artwork, illustrative, painterly, matte painting, highly detailed, cinematic composition
Negative :photo, photorealistic, realism, ugly

Fantasy Art 幻想艺术:
Prompt ：ethereal fantasy concept art of {prompt} . magnificent, celestial, ethereal, painterly, epic, majestic, magical, fantasy art, cover art, dreamy
Negative ：photographic, realistic, realism, 35mm film, dslr, cropped, frame, text, deformed, glitch, noise, noisy, off-center, deformed, cross-eyed, closed eyes, bad anatomy, ugly, disfigured, sloppy, duplicate, mutated, black and white
示例:
Prompt ：ethereal fantasy concept art of sorceress casting spells. magnificent, celestial, ethereal, painterly, epic, majestic, magical, fantasy art, cover art, dreamy
Negative ：photographic, realistic, realism, 35mm film, dslr, cropped, frame, text, deformed, glitch, noise, noisy, off-center, deformed, cross-eyed, closed eyes, bad anatomy, ugly, disfigured, sloppy, duplicate, mutated, black and white

Photography 图片风格:
Prompt ：Photographic of {prompt}. 35mm photograph, film, bokeh, professional, 4k, highly detailed
Negative :drawing, painting, crayon, sketch, graphite, impressionist, noisy, blurry, soft, deformed, ugly
示例:
Prompt ：Photographic of a construction worker looking down at city. 35mm photograph, film, bokeh, professional, 4k, highly detailed
Negative ：drawing, painting, crayon, sketch, graphite, impressionist, noisy, blurry, soft, deformed, ugly

Cinematic 电影风格:
Prompt ：cinematic film still {prompt} . shallow depth of field, vignette, highly detailed, high budget Hollywood movie, bokeh, cinemascope, moody
Negative ：anime, cartoon, graphic, text, painting, crayon, graphite, abstract, glitch, deformed, mutated, ugly, disfigured
示例:
Prompt ：cinematic film still, iron man. shallow depth of field, vignette, highly detailed, high budget Hollywood movie, bokeh, cinemascope, moody, epic, gorgeous, film grain, grainy
Negative ：anime, cartoon, graphic, text, painting, crayon, graphite, abstract, glitch, deformed, mutated, ugly, disfigured

Analog film 胶片电影滤镜风格:
Prompt ：analog film photo {prompt} . faded film, desaturated, 35mm photo, grainy, vignette, vintage, Kodachrome, Lomography, stained, highly detailed, found footage
Negative ：painting, drawing, illustration, glitch, deformed, mutated, cross-eyed, ugly, disfigured
示例:
Prompt ：analog film photo of old woman on the streets of london . faded film, desaturated, 35mm photo, grainy, vignette, vintage, Kodachrome, Lomography, stained, highly detailed, found footage
Negative ：painting, drawing, illustration, glitch, deformed, mutated, cross-eyed, ugly, disfigured

Neon Punk 明亮朋克风格:
Prompt ：neon Punk {prompt} . vibes, stunningly beautiful, crisp, detailed, sleek, ultramodern, high contrast, cinematic composition
Negative ：Blurry lines, Jagged lines, faded colors, faded images
示例:
Prompt ：NPai, 1990s sports car drifting through a jungle, hyperdetailed, maximalism, RAW photo, perfect aesthetic, grainy film, Fujifilm, textured skin, highly detailed and intricate
Negative ：Blurry lines, Jagged lines, faded colors, faded images

Craft Clay 黏土风格:
Prompt ：claymation style {prompt} . sculpture, clay art, centered composition, play-doh
Negative ：sloppy, messy, grainy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo, mutated
示例:
Prompt ：claymation style captain jack sparrow on tropical island. sculpture, clay art, centered composition, play-doh
Negative ：sloppy, messy, grainy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo, mutated

Prompt ：professional 3d model {prompt} . octane render, highly detailed, volumetric, dramatic lighting
Negative ：ugly, deformed, noisy, low poly, blurry, painting
示例:
Prompt ：professional 3d model bulky purple mecha with missiles. octane render, highly detailed, volumetric, dramatic lighting
Negative ：ugly, deformed, noisy, low poly, blurry, painting

Origami 折纸艺术:
Prompt ：origami style {prompt} . paper art, pleated paper, folded, origami art, pleats, cut and fold, centered composition
Negative ：noisy, sloppy, messy, grainy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo
示例:
Prompt ：origami style Winterfell. paper art, pleated paper, folded, origami art, pleats, cut and fold, centered composition
Negative ：noisy, sloppy, messy, grainy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo

Line Art 线稿艺术:
Prompt ：line art drawing {prompt} . professional, sleek, modern, minimalist, graphic, line art, vector graphics
Negative ：anime, photorealistic, 35mm film, deformed, glitch, blurry, noisy, off-center, deformed, cross-eyed, closed eyes, bad anatomy, ugly, disfigured, mutated, realism, realistic, impressionism, expressionism, oil, acrylic
示例:
Prompt ：line art of a kitchen in perspective. professional, sleek, modern, minimalist, graphic, line art, vector graphics
Negative ：anime, photorealistic, 35mm film, deformed, glitch, blurry, noisy, off-center, deformed, cross-eyed, closed eyes, bad anatomy, ugly, disfigured, mutated, realism, realistic, impressionism, expressionism, oil, acrylic

Pixel Art 像素艺术:
Prompt ：pixel-art {prompt} . low-res, blocky, pixel art style, 16-bit graphics
Negative ：sloppy, messy, blurry, noisy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo, realistic
示例:
Prompt ：16-bit pixel art, a cozy cafe side view, a beautiful day
Negative ：sloppy, messy, blurry, noisy, highly detailed, ultra textured, photo, realistic

Isometric Style 等距风格:
Prompt ：isometric style of {prompt}.low-res, blocky
Negative ：noisy, ultra textured, photo, realistic
示例:
Prompt ：isometric style of wizard working on spells. low-res, blocky
Negative ：noisy, ultra textured, photo, realistic

Comic Book 漫画风格:
Prompt ：comic book style {prompt}.professional detailed graphic novel illustration, intricate linework, intricate strokes
Negative ：anime face, ugly, old, anime style, cartoonish, anime face, photoreal, nude, (poorly drawn face, cross-eyed), sweaty skin, latex clothes, low fidelity
示例:
Prompt ：comic book style,Handsome man wearing a bulky fur coat, detailed hair, sitting in a well lit warm log wood cabin indoors, winter time, cozy scene, detailed reflective eyes, holding a book and lantern, warm lighting, (stormy snow window in the background), luxurious lodge, professional detailed graphic novel illustration, intricate linework, intricate strokes
Negative ：anime face, ugly, old, anime style, cartoonish, anime face, photoreal, nude, (poorly drawn face, cross-eyed), sweaty skin, latex clothes, low fidelity
